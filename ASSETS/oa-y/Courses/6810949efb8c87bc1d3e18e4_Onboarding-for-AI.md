- Module 1: Foundations of Artificial Intelligence (5 lessons)
- Module 2: Unlocking AI with APIs (3 lessons)
- Module 3: Mastering Prompt Engineering (3 lessons)
- Module 4: Automating Workflows with AI Triggers (3 lessons)
- Module 5: Integrating Webhooks into AI Workflows (3 lessons)
