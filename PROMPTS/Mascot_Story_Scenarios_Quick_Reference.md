## Scenario Summaries

### 1. Framework Implementation - "The Architect's Nest"
**Mascot:** Falcon  
**Activity:** Building systematically organized nest  
**Location:** Structured tree with hierarchical branches  
**Clothing Accent:** Blue architect's cap with measurements  
**Theme:** 3-tier file system architecture

---

### 2. RAG Systems - "The Knowledge Gatherer"
**Mascot:** Hummingbird  
**Activity:** Gathering nectar from organized flower sources  
**Location:** Structured garden with knowledge pathways  
**Clothing Accent:** Gold-rimmed librarian's glasses  
**Theme:** Dynamic knowledge retrieval systems

---

### 3. Ship Weak, Iterate - "The First Flight"
**Mascot:** Falcon  
**Activity:** Building improved nest with lessons learned  
**Location:** Two nests visible (old with notes, new improved)  
**Clothing Accent:** Builder's hardhat with version badges  
**Theme:** Iterative development philosophy

---

### 4. Early Adopters Only - "The Advanced Training Ground"
**Mascot:** Falcon  
**Activity:** Leading select group through advanced techniques  
**Location:** Specialized high-altitude training area  
**Clothing Accent:** Gold instructor's badge "Early Adopter"  
**Theme:** Focused training on ready individuals

---

### 5. Individual 121s - "The Personal Mentor"
**Mascot:** Chameleon  
**Activity:** One-on-one personalized guidance  
**Location:** Private, secluded training space  
**Clothing Accent:** Mentor's badge with individual focus symbol  
**Theme:** Personalized development over groups

---

### 6. Knowledge in Files - "The Library Builder"
**Mascot:** Shark  
**Activity:** Organizing knowledge into coral reef repository  
**Location:** Structured coral reef with compartments  
**Clothing Accent:** Librarian's satchel with organization tools  
**Theme:** Permanent knowledge storage culture

---

### 7. Automation & Integration - "The Connected Currents"

**Location:** Interconnected ocean current networks

**Theme:** n8n workflow automation

### 8. Performance Management - "The Efficiency Tracker"

## Theme to Scenario Quick Match
