## SMM-Specific Instructions

### Step 1: Read SMM Prompt Log
Read the SMM department prompt log file:
- `Dropbox/Nov25/SMM Nov25/SMM prompt log.md` (if exists)

**Time Scope:** Focus on this week's activities. When reading employee folders, include the current week's folders (e.g., if reporting on Nov 12, read folders 08, 09, 10, 11, 12), not just the single report day. This allows capturing the full context of ongoing work and multi-day tasks.

**Task Documentation Requirement:** Extract detailed task information from employee daily.md, plans.md, and task.md files. For each task mentioned, include:
- Task name and description
- Specific actions taken
- Outcomes achieved
- Impact or results

Avoid generic confirmations like "6 tasks completed" - instead provide specific summaries of WHAT tasks were accomplished.

### Step 2: Process Call Transcripts (if applicable)
When processing SMM-related call transcripts, focus on:

**SMM-Specific Sections to Emphasize:**

#### Section 3: ACTION ITEMS & TASKS
- **Content Tasks:** Social media content creation
- **Engagement Tasks:** Community management activities
- **Campaign Tasks:** Social media campaign execution
- **Analytics Tasks:** Performance tracking and reporting

#### Section 8: TOOLS & RESOURCES
- **Social Media Tools:** Platform management tools
- **Content Tools:** Content creation tools
- **Analytics Tools:** Performance tracking tools
- **AI Tools:** Content generation tools

#### Section 17: METRICS & PERFORMANCE INDICATORS
- **Engagement Metrics:** Likes, comments, shares, engagement rate
- **Reach Metrics:** Impressions, reach, follower growth
- **Conversion Metrics:** Click-through rate, conversion rate
- **Response Metrics:** Response time, response rate

#### Section 18: CREATIVE & CONTENT DELIVERABLES
- **Social Media Content:** Posts, stories, reels, videos
- **Campaign Content:** Campaign-specific content
- **Visual Assets:** Graphics, images, videos

### Step 3: Generate SMM Daily Activity Report

**File Location:** `/Nov25/SMM Nov25/Reports/{DAY}/Daily_Activity_Report_Nov{DAY}_2025.md`

**SMM-Specific Report Sections:**

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Report period (date)
   - Department: SMM
   - Team size: 2-3 employees
   - Total posts published
   - Total engagement interactions
   - Total campaigns active
   - Overall status

2. **Content Creation Activities**
   - Posts created and published
   - Content formats (posts, stories, reels, videos)
   - Content themes and topics
   - Content performance

3. **Community Management**
   - Comments responded to
   - Messages handled
   - Community engagement activities
   - Response time metrics

4. **Campaign Management**
   - Campaigns active
   - Campaign content created
   - Campaign performance
   - Campaign optimizations

5. **Platform Management**
   - Platforms managed (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube)
   - Platform-specific activities
   - Platform performance metrics
   - Platform optimizations

6. **Analytics & Performance**
   - Engagement metrics
   - Reach and impressions
   - Follower growth
   - Conversion metrics
   - Performance analysis

7. **Cross-Department Collaboration**
   - Collaboration with Design team (visual assets)
   - Collaboration with AI team (campaign alignment)
   - Collaboration with Content (content repurposing)
   - Collaboration with Sales team (lead generation)

8. **Metrics and Statistics**
   - Posts published: [count with platform breakdown and content types]
   - Engagement interactions: [count with interaction types]
   - Campaigns active: [count with campaign names and status]
   - Response time: [average response time]
   - Follower growth: [follower count changes by platform]

   **IMPORTANT:** For each metric, provide specific details rather than just numbers. Example:
   - Instead of: "Posts published: 15"
   - Write: "Posts published: 15 (Instagram: 5 posts including 2 reels; Facebook: 4 posts; LinkedIn: 3 professional posts; Twitter/X: 3 tweets; TikTok: 2 short videos)"

9. **Key Deliverables**
   - Social media content created
   - Campaign materials
   - Performance reports
   - Content calendar updates

10. **SMM Department Impact Analysis**
    - Impact level (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
    - Engagement improvements
    - Follower growth gains
    - Brand awareness increases
    - Categories affected (Content, Engagement, Campaigns, Analytics)

11. **SMM Achievements**
    - Content quality improvements
    - Engagement rate increases
    - Campaign successes
    - Community growth

12. **Challenges and Solutions**
    - Content creation challenges
    - Engagement issues
    - Platform algorithm changes
    - Solutions implemented

13. **Files Created/Modified Summary**
    - New social media content created (with paths)
    - Modified content files (with descriptions)
    - Campaign documentation updates
    - Analytics reports

14. **Recommendations for Follow-up**
    - Immediate actions required
    - Short-term improvements
    - Long-term enhancements

15. **Success Indicators**
    - Completed successfully (checkboxes)
    - Quality metrics (checkboxes)
    - Pending items

16. **Conclusion**
    - Summary of day's work
    - Key achievements recap
    - Impact assessment
    - Overall status
