## Department Context: Social Media Management

### Department Overview
- **Code:** SMM
- **Mission:** Social media strategy, content creation, community management, analytics
- **Team Size:** 2-3 employees
- **Key Responsibilities:**
  - Social media content creation for multiple platforms
  - Community engagement and management
  - Social media campaign execution
  - Performance analytics and reporting
  - Platform optimization

### Social Media Tools
**Content Creation:**
- Canva - Graphics and visual design
- CapCut - Video editing
- Adobe Suite - Advanced design

**Management & Scheduling:**
- Buffer, Hootsuite - Multi-platform management
- Later - Visual content planning

**Analytics:**
- Meta Business Suite - Facebook/Instagram
- LinkedIn Analytics, Twitter Analytics
- Google Analytics - Traffic tracking

**Platforms:**
- Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube
