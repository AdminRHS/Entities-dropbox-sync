## 4. Processes Library (Video Production Workflows)

### PROC-VID-001: Full Video Production Workflow

**Duration:** 5-10 days (typical client project)
**Owner:** Video team (Diana, Sviatoslav, Darya)

**Steps:**
1. **Client Brief** (Day 1)
   - Understand requirements, goals, audience
   - Review reference videos, style preferences
   - Define deliverables, timeline, budget

2. **Pre-Production** (Days 1-2)
   - Write script/storyboard
   - Create shot list
   - Gather assets (footage, graphics, music)
   - Organize project folder

3. **Production/Editing** (Days 3-7)
   - Import footage to Premiere/Resolve
   - Rough cut assembly
   - Fine cut editing
   - Add transitions, effects
   - Color correction/grading
   - Audio editing (music, SFX, voiceover)
   - Motion graphics (if needed - Diana)

4. **Review** (Days 7-8)
   - Internal review
   - Export draft version
   - Client review (Frame.io)
   - Collect feedback

5. **Revisions** (Days 8-9)
   - Implement client feedback
   - Fine-tune edits
   - Final color/audio pass

6. **Delivery** (Day 10)
   - Export final video (multiple formats if needed)
   - Upload to platform or deliver files
   - Create thumbnail (if YouTube)
   - Write description, tags

**Success Metrics:**
- On-time delivery: > 90%
- Client satisfaction: High (positive feedback)
- Revision rounds: ≤ 2 rounds typical

### PROC-VID-002: Social Media Video (Fast Turnaround)

**Duration:** 1-2 days
**Owner:** Video team

**Optimized for Speed:**
1. **Brief** (Hour 1) - Quick call/message with requirements
2. **Asset Gathering** (Hours 1-2) - Stock footage, music, graphics
3. **Editing** (Hours 3-8) - Fast-paced editing, trendy effects
4. **Review** (Hour 9) - Quick internal/client review
5. **Revisions** (Hour 10) - Minor tweaks
6. **Delivery** (Hour 11) - Export and upload

**Format Requirements:**
- Vertical: 9:16 (TikTok, Instagram Reels)
- Length: 15-60 seconds
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Subtitles/captions (auto-generated)

### PROC-VID-003: YouTube Video Production

**Duration:** 7-14 days
**Owner:** Video team

**YouTube-Specific Steps:**
1. **Concept & Script** - Plan engaging content
2. **Filming/Recording** - Shoot footage or screen record
3. **Editing** - Long-form editing (5-20 min videos)
4. **Thumbnail Design** - Eye-catching 1280x720px image
5. **SEO Optimization** - Title, description, tags for discovery
6. **Upload** - Schedule upload time
7. **Promotion** - Share on social media

**YouTube Best Practices:**
- Intro hook: First 10 seconds critical
- Pacing: Keep audience engaged
- Call-to-action: Subscribe, like, comment
- End screen: Suggest next video
