# Research Prompt: n8n-Similar Platforms Released in Recent Months

## Research Objective
Identify and analyze workflow automation platforms similar to n8n that have been released or announced in the last 3-6 months (approximately  - October 2025), with particular focus on solutions from major technology companies including OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and other prominent players in the automation/AI space.

## Research Scope

### Target Platform Characteristics
- **Workflow automation platforms** similar to n8n
- **No-code/low-code** visual workflow builders
- **API integration** and connector capabilities
- **Node-based** visual programming interfaces
- **Automation** and orchestration capabilities
- **Self-hosted** or cloud-based deployment options

### Key Companies to Research
1. **OpenAI** - Any workflow automation or agent orchestration platforms
2. **Google** - Google Cloud automation tools, Vertex AI workflows, or new releases
3. **Microsoft** - Power Automate updates, Azure Logic Apps, or new automation tools
4. **Amazon** - AWS Step Functions, EventBridge, or new automation services
5. **Other major tech companies** - Meta, Apple, Salesforce, etc.
6. **Startups and emerging platforms** - New entrants in the workflow automation space

### Time Frame
- **Primary focus**: Last 3-6 months (September 2024 - January 2025)
- **Secondary focus**: Major announcements or beta releases from the last 12 months that gained traction recently

## Research Questions

### 1. Platform Identification
- What are the names of recently released platforms similar to n8n?
- Which companies/developers created these platforms?
- What are their official websites and documentation URLs?

### 2. Release Information
- When were these platforms officially released or announced?
- What is their current status (beta, GA, preview)?
- Are they open-source, proprietary, or hybrid models?

### 3. Feature Comparison
For each identified platform, research:
- **Visual workflow builder**: Does it use node-based visual programming?
- **Integration capabilities**: What APIs and services can it connect to?
- **Deployment options**: Self-hosted, cloud, or both?
- **AI/ML features**: Any built-in AI capabilities or integrations?
- **Pricing model**: Free, freemium, or paid?
- **Target audience**: Enterprise, SMB, or individual developers?

### 4. Technical Specifications
- **Technology stack**: What technologies power the platform?
- **Architecture**: Microservices, monolithic, or serverless?
- **Scalability**: How does it handle large-scale workflows?
- **Security features**: Authentication, authorization, encryption capabilities

### 5. Market Positioning
- How do these platforms position themselves against n8n?
- What unique features differentiate them from n8n?
- What are their competitive advantages?

### 6. User Adoption & Community
- Early adopter feedback and reviews
- Community size and activity
- GitHub stars, forks, or other engagement metrics (if open-source)
- Documentation quality and developer resources
