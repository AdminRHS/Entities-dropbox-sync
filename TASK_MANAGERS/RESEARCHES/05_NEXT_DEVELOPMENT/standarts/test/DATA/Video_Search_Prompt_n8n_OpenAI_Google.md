### Relevance Score (0-100 points)

**Topic Alignment (0-40 points):**
- 40 pts: Deep dive into n8n, OpenAI, or Google Automation core topics
- 30 pts: Good coverage of one topic with mentions of others
- 20 pts: General automation tutorial
- 10 pts: Tangentially related
- 0 pts: Irrelevant

**Technical Depth (0-30 points):**
- 30 pts: Full code/workflow walkthrough, explains "why" and "how"
- 20 pts: Good demo but skips some details
- 10 pts: High-level overview
- 0 pts: Marketing fluff

**Actionability (0-30 points):**
- 30 pts: **MUST HAVE:** Includes downloadable resources (JSON, Code) AND clear steps
- 20 pts: Clear steps but no resources (manual recreation required)
- 10 pts: Vague instructions
- 0 pts: Impossible to replicate
