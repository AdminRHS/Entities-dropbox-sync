## üìã Research Best Practices

### **DO:**
‚úÖ **Focus on implementation details** - Look for videos showing actual setup, not just overviews
‚úÖ **Prioritize small team solutions** - Prefer tools designed for 1-10 person teams

‚úÖ **Consider EU compliance** - Always check GDPR compatibility
‚úÖ **Map to current tasks** - Connect discoveries to HR-001, HR-004, etc.

‚úÖ **Look for integrations** - Prioritize tools that work with current stack
‚úÖ **Capture resources** - Download workflow exports, templates, scripts
‚úÖ **Share quickly** - Distribute high-impact findings immediately
‚úÖ **Track trends** - Notice patterns across multiple videos
‚úÖ **Ask questions** - Engage with video creators if clarification needed

‚ùå **Ignore pricing** - Always check costs and free tier limitations
‚ùå **Overlook compliance** - Don't recommend tools that can't meet EU/GDPR requirements

‚ùå **Batch documentation** - Document immediately, not later
