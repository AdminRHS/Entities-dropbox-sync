## Assignment Guidelines

### When Assigning Video Processing:

1. **Check video content topic** (AI, Design, Development, Lead Gen, etc.)
2. **Assign to department expert when possible**
   - Lead Gen video → Lead Gen employee
   - AI tool video → AI employee
   - Design video → Design employee
3. **Fallback to Video or AI department** if no department expert available
4. **Provide clear examples** from completed videos (Video_002, Video_009)
5. **Set realistic deadlines:**
   - Phase 5-7 (1-1.5 hours) → 3-5 days
   - Complete processing (2-3 hours) → 5-7 days

### Materials to Provide:

- Video transcription path
- PMT-009 prompt (Phase 5-7)
- Example output (Video_002_Gap_Analysis.md, Video_002_Library_Mapping_Report.md)
- LIBRARIES access (for checking existing entries)
