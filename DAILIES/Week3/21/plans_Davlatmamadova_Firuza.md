1. Work with Job Sites

Identify the most relevant job boards for your industry (e.g., LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, niche boards).

Set up saved searches and alerts for new opportunities.

Update and optimize your profile and resume on each site.

Track applications and follow-ups in a simple spreadsheet or CRM.

2. Work with gScrape

Use gScrape to collect targeted leads based on industry, location, or job role.

Clean and organize the scraped data so it’s ready for outreach.

Tag leads by priority (hot, warm, cold) for more strategic messaging.

3. Record a Video Demonstrating How to Book a Call

Create a short step-by-step walkthrough showing prospects how to schedule a call with you.

Include:

The link to your calendar

A quick explanation of the call’s purpose

What they can expect after booking

Add the video to your outreach messages, website, and social profiles.

4. Record a Video Explaining How to Message a Lead

Make a video tutorial showing how to send effective outreach messages.

Cover topics such as:

Personalizing the message

Avoiding spammy language

Offering value

Clear call-to-action

Use this video for onboarding or training others on your team.

5. Message Old Connections

Re-engage with previous contacts by sending personalized messages.

Mention past interactions or shared interests to make it natural.

Update them on what you’re doing now and create an opening for a conversation or call.

Add any engaged contacts to your follow-up pipeline.
