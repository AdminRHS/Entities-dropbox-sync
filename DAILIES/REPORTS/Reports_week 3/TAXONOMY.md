# REPORTS Entity Taxonomy

## Overview
The REPORTS entity contains daily, weekly, and ad-hoc reports generated by various departments and systems.

## IDS System
- **Prefix:** `RPT`
- **ID Format:** `RPT-[YYYY]-[ID]` (e.g., `RPT-2025-001`) or `RPT-[ID]` depending on volume. Let's stick to `RPT-[ID]` for simplicity as per standard, or `RPT-[Date]` for daily reports?
- **Standard:** `RPT-[ID]` (Sequential)

## Classification
- **Daily Reports:** `RPT-DLY`
- **Weekly Reports:** `RPT-WKY`
- **Ad-hoc Reports:** `RPT-ADH`

## Naming Convention
`[Prefix]-[Type]_[Department]_[Date]_[Description].ext`

**Examples:**
- `RPT-DLY_Sales_2025-11-19_Daily_Activity.md`
- `RPT-WKY_Dev_2025-W47_Sprint_Summary.md`

## Directory Structure
- `DATA/`: Raw report data (JSON, CSV).
- `DOCS/`: Templates and guides for reporting.
- `ARCHIVE/`: Older reports.
