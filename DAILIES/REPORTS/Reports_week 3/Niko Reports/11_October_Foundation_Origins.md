## 11. Lessons Learned

### 11.1 Systematic Approach

> "Documenting processes before implementation saves time"

- RACI matrices prevent confusion
- Standardized tools reduce training
- Version control enables accountability

### 11.2 AI Integration

> "MCP provides better integration than individual AI tools"

- Target high-impact repetitive tasks
- Maintain human oversight
- Plan before implementing

### 11.3 Team Development

> "Training must be systematic and measurable"

- Knowledge sharing prevents single points of failure
- Performance tracking enables improvement
- Onboarding must be comprehensive

### 11.4 Scalability

> "Microservices enable independent development"

- API-first improves integration
- Monitoring is essential
- Architecture before features
