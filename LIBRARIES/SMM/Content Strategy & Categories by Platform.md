## **DESIGNER-FOCUSED PLATFORMS: Comprehensive Strategy**

### **1\. Instagram: The Visual Community Hub**

#### **Strategic Implementation**

**Platform Philosophy**: Be a generous expert. Teach, inspire, connect on a human level.

**Posting Strategy**:

* **Frequency**: 3-5 feed posts/week \+ 1-3 Stories daily  
* **Optimal Times**: Weekdays 11am-1pm, 7-9pm EST  
* **Content Mix**:  
  * 35% Educational Carousels  
  * 25% Process Reels  
  * 20% Portfolio Showcases  
  * 10% Behind-the-Scenes Stories  
  * 10% Community Engagement

#### **Content Categories & Execution**

**1\. The Educational Carousel**

* **Format**: 5-10 slides, 1080×1350px (4:5 ratio)  
* **Examples**:  
  * "5 Dark Patterns to Avoid in Your UI" (Market Conduct principles)  
  * "A Designer's Guide to Creative Commons" (CC licensing explanation)  
  * "Accessibility 101: Color Contrast" (DEI commitment)  
  * "AI as Co-Pilot: 5 Steps to Better Logo Design"

**2\. The Process Reel**

* **Format**: 15-90 seconds, 1080×1920px (9:16 ratio)  
* **Examples**:  
  * Time-lapse illustration creation  
  * Before/After UI transformation  
  * "How I Use AI" demonstrations  
  * Design challenge solutions

**3\. Interactive Stories**

* **"Ask a Designer" Q\&A sessions**  
* **Polls**: "Which design do you prefer?"  
* **Quizzes**: Test design knowledge  
* **Behind-the-scenes**: Daily studio life

#### **AI-Focused Content Strategy**

* **Transparency First**: Always show human input → AI assistance → human refinement  
* **Never Raw AI**: Present only human-curated, refined outputs  
* **Process Documentation**: Screen recordings showing actual workflow  
* **Educational Angle**: Teach others how to integrate AI ethically

#### **Algorithm Optimization**

* **Hashtags**: 3-5 highly relevant, niche-specific tags  
* **Keywords**: Optimize captions, on-screen text, bio  
* **Engagement Triggers**: Questions in captions, interactive stickers  
* **Saves & Shares**: Create reference-worthy content (5x algorithm weight)

### **2\. Behance: The Professional Portfolio Platform**

#### **Strategic Implementation**

**Platform Philosophy**: Your digital gallery and professional archive.

**Content Strategy**:

* **Frequency**: 1 comprehensive project/month  
* **Project Structure**:  
  1. Challenge Definition (business problem)  
  2. Ethical Framework ("Guiding Principles" section)  
  3. Process Documentation (sketches, iterations)  
  4. Inclusive Design Features (accessibility details)  
  5. Solution Presentation (high-res mockups)  
  6. Impact Metrics (results, testimonials)

#### **AI Integration Showcase**

**Project Template**: "AI as Creative Partner: \[Brand Name\] Case Study"

* Project brief & human strategy  
* Moodboarding & concepting  
* AI-assisted ideation (with prompts)  
* Human curation & refinement  
* Final brand system & guidelines

#### **Technical Specifications**

* **Images**: 1400px wide (max 2800px), 50MB max, JPEG format  
* **Videos**: Embed from YouTube/Vimeo for full-width display  
* **Project Cover**: 808×632px (critical for clicks)

### **3\. Dribbble: High-Fidelity Showcase**

#### **Strategic Implementation**

**Platform Philosophy**: Demonstrate technical craft and aesthetic mastery.

**Content Strategy**:

* **Frequency**: 2-4 shots/week  
* **Format**: 1600×1200px (4:3 ratio), 10MB max  
* **Focus**: UI components, animations, micro-interactions

#### **AI Content Approach**

* **Shot Example**: "Human-designed UI, AI-generated data visualizations"  
* **Description**: Brief process explanation emphasizing human strategy  
* **Animation**: Show AI-assisted workflow in 24-second MP4

### **4\. Pinterest: Long-Term Discovery Engine**
