# TAXONOMY ANALYSIS

## Workflows Identified

### WORKFLOW 1: Cross-Platform Content Distribution

**OBJECTIVE**: Maximize content reach and engagement by systematically distributing and repurposing content across multiple social media platforms

**STEPS**:
1. Create long-form primary content (YouTube tutorial, Blog article, or Behance project)
2. Extract key segments for short-form platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels)
3. Create visual summaries for image-focused platforms (Instagram feed, Pinterest)
4. Develop quick tips and threads for text platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn)
5. Build community discussions on engagement platforms (Facebook Groups, Discord)

**DURATION**: 3-6 hours for complete distribution cycle

**COMPLEXITY**: High

**TOOLS USED**:
- YouTube (long-form video)
- Instagram (Reels, Feed posts, Stories)
- TikTok (short-form video)
- LinkedIn (articles, posts)
- Twitter/X (threads, tweets)
- Pinterest (pins, infographics)
- Facebook (groups, posts)
- Blog platforms (Medium, Substack)
- Behance (portfolio projects)

**INPUT**: Single piece of primary content (video, article, or project)

**OUTPUT**:
- 1 long-form piece
- 3-5 short-form variations
- 5-10 visual summaries
- 10-15 micro-content pieces
- Multiple discussion threads

**PLATFORM**: Multi-platform

**USE CASE**: Content marketing, brand awareness, audience growth across platforms
