 **Task Template:**

**Name:** Communicate with Remote Candidates via Instagram DMs

**Description:** Facilitate communication with candidates through Instagram DMs, covering vacancy confirmation, language proficiency, availability for interviews, and rescheduling as needed, ensuring a smooth recruitment process.

**Profession:** Recruiter

### **Step Templates:**

#### **1\. Confirm Interest in Vacancies**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Vacancies**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Verify Vacancy Interest  
    * **Guide:** Begin by confirming the specific vacancy the candidate is interested in and provide an overview of the role. Format: Text

#### **2\. Assess Language Proficiency for Communications**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Communications**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Inquire About Language Skills  
    * **Guide:** Ask candidates about their language skills, specifying the level of proficiency needed for the role. Format: Text

#### **3\. Confirm Availability for Job Interviews**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Interviews**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Check Interview Timing Preferences  
    * **Guide:** Discuss potential interview timings with candidates to identify a convenient slot. Offer alternatives if needed. Format: Text  
  * **Name:** Confirm Final Interview Time  
    * **Guide:** Once an agreeable time is determined, confirm the appointment details and provide the link for the virtual interview. Format: Text

#### **4\. Reschedule Job Interviews if Necessary**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Interviews**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Handle Rescheduling Requests  
    * **Guide:** If the candidate requests a change, negotiate a new interview time that suits both parties. Ensure timely updates to all involved. Format: Text

#### **5\. Provide Pre-Interview Information for Job Requests**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Job Requests**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Send Pre-Interview Instructions  
    * **Guide:** Address any candidate questions regarding interview preparation. Provide specific instructions if necessary, such as expected topics or general format. Format: Text

#### **6\. Follow Up on Candidate Profiles for Job Applications**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Job Applications**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Request Resume Submission  
    * **Guide:** Ask the candidate to submit their resume via an alternate platform (e.g., Telegram) if it hasnâ€™t been provided yet. Confirm receipt before the interview. Format: Text

#### **7\. Confirm Interest in Vacancies Post-Travel or Delay**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Vacancies**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Reconfirm Interest in the Role  
    * **Guide:** If a candidate previously indicated a delay, follow up to confirm ongoing interest in the vacancy and potential readiness for the next steps. Format: Text

2\.

**Task Template:**

**Name:** Screen Candidates via Instagram DMs

**Description:** Engage with candidates responding to Instagram ads by confirming their application details, assessing language proficiency, and requesting relevant application materials before scheduling an interview.

**Profession:** Recruiter

### **Step Templates:**

#### **1\. Confirm Candidate Interest in Vacancies**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Vacancies**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Verify Vacancy Interest  
    * **Guide:** Begin the conversation by greeting the candidate and confirming which specific vacancy they are interested in. This ensures clarity on the role they are applying for. Format: Text

#### **2\. Assess Language Skills for Communications**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Communications**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Inquire About Language Proficiency  
    * **Guide:** Ask the candidate about their proficiency level in the required language(s) for the role. Provide a framework for response, such as beginner, intermediate, or advanced, and ask for any relevant language certifications or experiences. Format: Text

#### **3\. Request Candidate Profiles via CV or Video Business Card**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Profiles**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Request CV or Video Introduction  
    * **Guide:** Ask the candidate to share a CV or a video business card introducing themselves and summarizing their professional background. Provide instructions for sending the file via Instagram or another platform, if needed. Format: Text

#### **4\. Schedule Interviews for Job Requests**

* **Tool:** Instagram  
* **Object:** **Job Requests**  
* **Checklist Items:**  
  * **Name:** Confirm Interview Availability  
    * **Guide:** Once you have gathered their language proficiency and CV or video business card, confirm that the candidate is interested in proceeding. If they are, request their availability for a virtual interview. Format: Text
