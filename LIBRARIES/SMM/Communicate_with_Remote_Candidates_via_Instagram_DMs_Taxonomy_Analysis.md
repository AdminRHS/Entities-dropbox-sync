## Full Document Content Extraction

### Task Template 1: Communicate with Remote Candidates via Instagram DMs

**Purpose**: Facilitate ongoing communication with candidates through Instagram DMs covering all stages from initial contact to interview scheduling.

**Profession**: Recruiter

#### Step-by-Step Process:

**Step 1: Confirm Interest in Vacancies**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Vacancies
- **Action**: Verify Vacancy Interest
- **Guide**: Begin by confirming the specific vacancy the candidate is interested in and provide an overview of the role.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 2: Assess Language Proficiency for Communications**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Communications
- **Action**: Inquire About Language Skills
- **Guide**: Ask candidates about their language skills, specifying the level of proficiency needed for the role.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 3: Confirm Availability for Job Interviews**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Interviews
- **Actions**:
  - Check Interview Timing Preferences
  - Confirm Final Interview Time
- **Guide**:
  - Discuss potential interview timings with candidates to identify a convenient slot. Offer alternatives if needed.
  - Once an agreeable time is determined, confirm the appointment details and provide the link for the virtual interview.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 4: Reschedule Job Interviews if Necessary**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Interviews
- **Action**: Handle Rescheduling Requests
- **Guide**: If the candidate requests a change, negotiate a new interview time that suits both parties. Ensure timely updates to all involved.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 5: Provide Pre-Interview Information for Job Requests**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Job Requests
- **Action**: Send Pre-Interview Instructions
- **Guide**: Address any candidate questions regarding interview preparation. Provide specific instructions if necessary, such as expected topics or general format.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 6: Follow Up on Candidate Profiles for Job Applications**
- **Tool**: Instagram (primary), Telegram (alternate)
- **Object**: Job Applications
- **Action**: Request Resume Submission
- **Guide**: Ask the candidate to submit their resume via an alternate platform (e.g., Telegram) if it hasn't been provided yet. Confirm receipt before the interview.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 7: Confirm Interest in Vacancies Post-Travel or Delay**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Vacancies
- **Action**: Reconfirm Interest in the Role
- **Guide**: If a candidate previously indicated a delay, follow up to confirm ongoing interest in the vacancy and potential readiness for the next steps.
- **Format**: Text

### Task Template 2: Screen Candidates via Instagram DMs

**Purpose**: Engage with candidates responding to Instagram ads by confirming application details, assessing qualifications, and scheduling interviews.

**Profession**: Recruiter

#### Step-by-Step Process:

**Step 1: Confirm Candidate Interest in Vacancies**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Vacancies
- **Action**: Verify Vacancy Interest
- **Guide**: Begin the conversation by greeting the candidate and confirming which specific vacancy they are interested in. This ensures clarity on the role they are applying for.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 2: Assess Language Skills for Communications**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Communications
- **Action**: Inquire About Language Proficiency
- **Guide**: Ask the candidate about their proficiency level in the required language(s) for the role. Provide a framework for response, such as beginner, intermediate, or advanced, and ask for any relevant language certifications or experiences.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 3: Request Candidate Profiles via CV or Video Business Card**
- **Tool**: Instagram (with alternative platform option)
- **Object**: Profiles
- **Action**: Request CV or Video Introduction
- **Guide**: Ask the candidate to share a CV or a video business card introducing themselves and summarizing their professional background. Provide instructions for sending the file via Instagram or another platform, if needed.
- **Format**: Text

**Step 4: Schedule Interviews for Job Requests**
- **Tool**: Instagram
- **Object**: Job Requests
- **Action**: Confirm Interview Availability
- **Guide**: Once you have gathered their language proficiency and CV or video business card, confirm that the candidate is interested in proceeding. If they are, request their availability for a virtual interview.
- **Format**: Text

# TAXONOMY ANALYSIS

## Workflows Identified

### WORKFLOW 1: Communicate with Remote Candidates via Instagram DMs

**OBJECTIVE**: Facilitate comprehensive communication with job candidates through Instagram DMs, covering all stages from vacancy confirmation to interview scheduling and follow-up

**STEPS**:
1. Confirm interest in vacancies
2. Assess language proficiency for communications
3. Confirm availability for job interviews
4. Reschedule job interviews if necessary
5. Provide pre-interview information for job requests
6. Follow up on candidate profiles for job applications
7. Confirm interest in vacancies post-travel or delay

**DURATION**: 20-40 minutes per candidate (across multiple interactions)

**COMPLEXITY**: Medium

**TOOLS USED**:
- Instagram (Direct Messages)
- Telegram (for resume/file sharing)

**INPUT**: Initial candidate inquiry or ongoing candidate relationship

**OUTPUT**:
- Confirmed interview schedule
- Collected candidate materials (CV/resume)
- Maintained candidate engagement throughout process

**PLATFORM**: Instagram (primary), Telegram (secondary)

**USE CASE**: Remote recruitment, ongoing candidate relationship management

---

**STEPS**:
1. Confirm candidate interest in vacancies
2. Assess language skills for communications
3. Request candidate profiles via CV or video business card
4. Schedule interviews for job requests

**DURATION**: 15-25 minutes per candidate (single screening session)

**COMPLEXITY**: Medium

**TOOLS USED**:
- Instagram (Direct Messages)
- Video recording tools (for video business cards)
- Alternative platforms (Telegram, email) for file sharing

**INPUT**: Candidate response to Instagram recruitment advertisement

**OUTPUT**:
- Qualified candidate profile
- Scheduled virtual interview
- Collected application materials (CV or video business card)

**PLATFORM**: Instagram (primary)

**USE CASE**: Initial candidate screening from social media advertising campaigns

**PURPOSE**: Comprehensive candidate communication and document collection

**TOOLS INTEGRATED**:
- Instagram (primary communication)
- Telegram (file sharing)

**FLOW**:
```
Initial screening (Instagram DMs) ‚Üí

Provide Telegram contact ‚Üí
Candidate sends resume (Telegram) ‚Üí
```

- Instagram excels at conversational communication but has limitations for reliable document transfer

## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### PLATFORM: Instagram

**BEST PRACTICE 1: Use DMs for Initial Screening**

**What to Do**: Conduct initial candidate screening through Instagram Direct Messages rather than formal application forms

**Reason**:
- Creates informal, comfortable first contact
- Reduces barriers to candidate engagement
- Candidates already familiar with Instagram messaging
- Allows quick back-and-forth qualification conversations
- Higher response rate than formal application processes

**Context**: Workflow 2, Step 1-2 (Screening candidates responding to Instagram ads)

**Evidence**: Both Task Templates emphasize Instagram DM as primary communication channel for all initial interactions

---

**BEST PRACTICE 2: Provide Multiple Contact Options for Document Submission**

**What to Do**: When requesting CVs or documents, offer Instagram AND alternate platform (Telegram) options

**Reason**:
- Instagram file sharing can be unreliable for professional documents
- Gives candidates flexibility in submission method
- Reduces friction in document collection process
- Ensures document quality and deliverability

**Context**: Workflow 1, Step 6; Workflow 2, Step 3
